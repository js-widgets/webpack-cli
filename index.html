Quick Introduction - 5min ⏱️

Configurable

Build once and embed many times. Configure your widgets when embedding to leverage the same widget in multiple situations. "Tweak the widget knobs and stamp".

Modular

Widgets are designed to work seamlessly with each other in any digital experience. Use them in a website, or an application, as your digital building blocks.

Consistent

Weather your organization uses a CMS, static HTML, or any other tech widgets stay the same. Feel free to re-platform while keeping your widgets intact. Leverage the CMS integrations for a better editorial experience.
